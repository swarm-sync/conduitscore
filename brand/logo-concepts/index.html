ConduitScore Logo Directions

Five cleaner directions built against the Gemini visual language: dark spectral surfaces, bold Syne typography, and a focused palette of Spectra Red, Neural Purple, and Cyber Lime. These are intentionally simple and favicon-friendly. No live site branding has been changed.

Option 1

Aperture C

The strongest balance of brand and function. It reads as a bold C immediately, then reveals score bars inside the conduit.

Option 2

Signal Path

More directional and kinetic. This one leans into the idea of data entering the conduit and leaving as proof.

Option 3

Score Bars C

The bluntest, most dashboard-native option. It will survive tiny favicon sizes better than almost anything here.

Option 4

Target Cut

The most audit-oriented concept. Precision, center point, and scan geometry without slipping into generic cyber art.

Option 5

CS Monogram

The most emblematic and ownable. This is the option if you want the brand to feel like a product badge, not just a tool name.
