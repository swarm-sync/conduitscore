# Twitter/X — Submission Guide

## Thread Approach
1. Compose the thread using thread.md content
2. Post Tweet 1 first, then reply to yourself with each subsequent tweet
3. Add a screenshot of a real scan result to Tweet 1 (improves reach significantly)
4. Tag any brands you've scanned (only if the score data is positive or constructive)

## Brand Scan Strategy (highest-engagement approach)
1. Pick 5-10 recognizable sites to scan (SaaS tools, SEO tools, marketing agencies)
2. Post the scan result for each with commentary
3. This creates ongoing content AND drives curiosity about their own scores

## Timing
- Best: Tuesday–Thursday, 9–11 AM or 2–4 PM ET
- Pin your launch tweet to your profile

## Notes
- Without API credentials, posts must be created and published manually
- Screenshots of scan results are more shareable than text-only posts
- Tag @conduitscore if that account exists — builds brand presence
