| Agency outreach | 5+ responses from 20 sent | Email replies |
| Weekly Active Scanners | 40+ | App analytics |
| Free→paid conversion | 2+ paying customers | Stripe dashboard |

Double down on whichever channel hits its Week 1 target first.
