# Hacker News — Show HN Submission Guide

## Prerequisites
- [ ] HN account in good standing (karma > 0, not shadowbanned)
- [ ] Post during peak HN hours (see timing below)
- [ ] Product must be live and accessible

## Submission Steps

1. Go to https://news.ycombinator.com/submit
2. **Title** (paste from show-hn-title.txt):
   `Show HN: ConduitScore – scan any website for AI-agent visibility (0-100 score)`
3. **URL**: `https://conduitscore.com`
4. Leave the text field blank (HN guidelines prefer URL-only for Show HN with a live product)
5. If you include text, paste show-hn-body.md content — but the first comment approach is better

## Alternative: Comment-First Approach
1. Submit with URL only (no body text)
2. Immediately post show-hn-body.md as your first comment
3. This gets more engagement because commenters see your context

## Timing
- **Best time**: Tuesday–Thursday, 8–10 AM ET (US East Coast work morning)
- **Avoid**: Fridays after 2 PM ET, weekends, and any day a major tech story is breaking
- HN front page has ~24 hours of viability for a strong post

## HN Rules — Strictly Follow
- NO marketing language: no "powerful," "seamless," "game-changing," "innovative"
- Title must start with "Show HN:" for original projects
- Be ready to respond to technical questions in the comments — HN rewards technical depth
- Do not ask friends to upvote — HN's fraud detection is sophisticated
- If asked about methodology, answer with specifics and acknowledge limitations honestly

## Expected Outcomes
- A good Show HN can generate 50–300 signups in 24 hours
- Technical criticism is normal and valuable — treat it as free user research
- Top HN posts in the SEO/AI space regularly reach front page — this topic has current relevance
