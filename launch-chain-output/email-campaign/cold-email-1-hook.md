**Send Day:** Day 0 (Monday or Tuesday, 9–11am recipient's local timezone)
**Audience:** SEO agency owners and directors (2–20 employees)
**Framework:** PAS — Problem → Agitate → Solution

## Subject Lines

**Primary (Subject A):** [CONTACT_NAME] — ran your site through something
**Variant (Subject B):** Your AI visibility score: [INSERT_THEIR_ACTUAL_SCORE]/100

*Subject A rationale: Personalization + intrigue combo — recipient sees their name and a vague but intriguing hook, drives curiosity opens. Predicted open rate: 38–45% for cold outreach at this personalization level.*

*Subject B rationale: Direct data lead — shows the score immediately in subject, creates instant anxiety/curiosity. Use for prospects where score is below 40 for maximum impact.*

## Personalization Placeholder Instructions

Before sending this email, you MUST:
1. Run a ConduitScore scan on `[THEIR_WEBSITE]` at conduitscore.com
2. Record the actual score in `[INSERT_THEIR_ACTUAL_SCORE]`
3. If score < 50: identify the top 2 issues from the scan report and fill `[ISSUE_1]` and `[ISSUE_2]`
4. If score 50–75: use the "ahead of median" copy block instead
5. Replace `[CONTACT_NAME]` with prospect's first name (LinkedIn-sourced)
6. Replace `[THEIR_WEBSITE]` with the prospect's agency domain
7. Replace `[YOUR_NAME]` with your name (Ben Stone or sender name)
8. Replace `[AGENCY_NAME]` with the prospect's agency name if known

**Required:** Do not send Email 1 without the real scan score. The score is the hook. A placeholder is not acceptable.
