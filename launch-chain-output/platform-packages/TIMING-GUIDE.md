# ConduitScore — Launch Timing Guide

## Pre-Launch (Days -14 to -1)

**Day -14 to -7: Foundation**
- [ ] Verify conduitscore.com is fully live and stable
- [ ] Seed 15-20 brand scans (scan recognizable SaaS sites, screenshot results)
- [ ] Prepare agency outreach list (20-30 SEO/digital agencies)
- [ ] Write 20 personalized cold emails with agency site scans attached
- [ ] Set up UTM parameter tracking for all launch channels
- [ ] Draft Product Hunt listing (upload to PH dashboard, set to "not yet launched")
- [ ] Prepare all social media assets (scan result screenshots, OG images)
- [ ] Submit to BetaList and Betapage (these have 3-7 day review queues)

**Day -5 to -1: Pre-Launch Seeding**
- [ ] Send first 5 agency cold emails (test response rate before sending full batch)
- [ ] Post first Reddit content (r/SEO or r/webdev) — educational post, not launch announcement
- [ ] Post Twitter/X brand scan thread for 1-2 recognizable sites
- [ ] Submit to BetaList if not already (3-7 day approval queue)

## Launch Week (Days 1-7)

**Day 1 — Agency Outreach Wave**
- [ ] Send remaining 15-25 agency cold emails
- [ ] Post Reddit r/SEO "I analyzed 500 websites" post
- [ ] Share HN Show HN post (timing: 8-10 AM ET Tuesday or Wednesday)
- [ ] Share on Twitter/X — full launch thread
- [ ] Post on LinkedIn (Version A or B from linkedin/post.md)
- [ ] Email any beta users / early subscribers

**HN Conditional Logic:**
- IF the Show HN reaches front page (top 10 within 2 hours): hold back Product Hunt 1 week to maximize HN momentum
- IF the Show HN does not trend: proceed with Product Hunt on Day 3-4

**Day 2-3: Double Down**
- [ ] Respond to all comments on Reddit and HN
- [ ] Post 3-5 more brand scan results on Twitter/X (tag the companies)
- [ ] Send pitch emails to TechCrunch and Wired
- [ ] Submit to Slashdot (lower priority)

**Day 4-5: Product Hunt**
- [ ] Launch on Product Hunt (Tuesday/Wednesday 12:01 AM PST)
- [ ] Post first comment immediately (first-comment.md)
- [ ] Share PH link personally on all channels
- [ ] Engage every PH comment within 2 hours

**Day 6-7: Consolidate**
- [ ] Submit dev.to and Hashnode articles (set canonical URLs correctly)
- [ ] Send follow-up to agencies who responded but haven't converted
- [ ] Analyze 48-hour leading indicators (scan/signup ratio, fix gate CTR, return rate)

## Week 2 — Content & Amplification

- [ ] Publish HackerNoon article
- [ ] Publish Medium article
- [ ] Post Indie Hackers launch post
- [ ] Pitch freeCodeCamp (if you have 3+ published samples — prerequisite)
- [ ] Publish "AI Visibility Rankings" for a specific vertical (top 50 SaaS tools)
  → This is an SEO asset + earned media bait; publish on your own blog first
- [ ] Send VentureBeat guest post pitch
- [ ] Review conversion data and double down on highest-performing channel

## Week 3-4 — Scaling

- [ ] Product Hunt launch (if not done in Week 1)
- [ ] AppSumo application (only after 100+ paying customers)
- [ ] Begin LinkedIn/Twitter brand scan series (one scan per day for 30 days)
- [ ] Follow up on press pitches

## Canonical URL Chain
When cross-posting articles to multiple platforms, set canonical URLs in this order:
1. Your own blog (conduitscore.com/blog/) — primary canonical
2. freeCodeCamp (if published there)
3. Medium
4. HackerNoon
5. dev.to
6. Hashnode

Each platform should point its canonical URL field to the one above it in this hierarchy.
Failure to do this causes duplicate content SEO penalties.
