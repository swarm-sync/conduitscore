# Cold Email 4 — The Objection Handler
**Sequence:** Agency Cold Outreach
**Send Day:** Day 7 (the following Tuesday, 9–11am recipient's local timezone)
**Audience:** SEO agency owners and directors (2–20 employees)
**Framework:** PAS — Problem → Agitate → Solution (objection-reframe variant)

---

## Subject Lines

**Primary (Subject A):** "My clients don't know about AI search yet"
**Variant (Subject B):** ChatGPT hit 200M weekly users. Your clients will ask soon.

*Subject A rationale: Quoting the objection verbatim in the subject is a pattern-interrupt — prospects who have had this thought will recognize themselves and open to see what comes next. Predicted open rate: 30–38%.*

*Subject B rationale: Stakes-raising data — positions the reader as potentially behind a trend that is already mainstream. Good for prospects who have been slow to respond.*

---

## Preview Text

Hey [CONTACT_NAME],

The most common thing I hear from agencies when I talk about AI visibility: "My clients aren't asking about this yet."

Two thoughts on that:

**1. They will.** ChatGPT has 200M weekly active users. Perplexity processes 100M+ queries/month. Google AI Overviews appear on 25% of US searches. The clients who start asking in the next 6 months will go to the agency that can answer first — with data.

**2. You can introduce the conversation.** Run a free scan on your next client call. Show them a score of 34/100. Say: "Google can find you. ChatGPT probably can't. Here's why." The score creates the conversation.

The agencies winning right now aren't waiting for clients to ask. They're showing clients they have a problem they didn't know existed.

That's the sales motion that wins new retainers — not waiting to respond to a request.

Quick question: are any of your current clients in SaaS, e-commerce, or content publishing? Those are the verticals where AI visibility problems are most acute right now.

— [YOUR_NAME]
