# AppSumo — Submission Guide

## About AppSumo
AppSumo features lifetime deals for SaaS tools. Selling a lifetime deal sacrifices recurring revenue but produces fast user acquisition, reviews, and word-of-mouth. Best suited as a time-limited campaign.

## Prerequisite: Product Requirements
- [ ] Live product with proven traction (typically 50+ paying customers or strong free-tier usage)
- [ ] Ability to honor lifetime deal users long-term
- [ ] Product mature enough to support a large sudden user influx

## Submission
1. Apply at: https://sell.appsumo.com/
2. Complete the application form with:
   - Product name: ConduitScore
   - Category: SEO / AI Tools
   - Current MRR: [your current number]
   - Free trial: Yes (free tier available)
   - Unique value: Only dedicated AI visibility scorer; 7-analyzer methodology
3. AppSumo will respond within 2-3 weeks
4. If accepted, negotiate deal terms (typically 70% revenue share to AppSumo)

## Deal Structure Recommendation
- Lifetime deal: 3 tiers
  - Tier 1 ($49 one-time): Starter-equivalent (50 scans/mo, all fixes)
  - Tier 2 ($99 one-time): Pro-equivalent (100 scans/mo + projects)
  - Tier 3 ($199 one-time): Growth-equivalent (500 scans/mo + API)

## When to Submit
- After: 100+ paying customers, established product stability
- AppSumo works best as a one-time acquisition burst, not a primary revenue channel
- Do not submit AppSumo and ProductHunt in the same month — focus attention on one
