Hey [CONTACT_NAME],

Whether or not you looked at ConduitScore yet — here's what our scan data shows about what actually moves AI visibility scores:

**1. Allowing AI crawlers in robots.txt (+12 pts)**
35.7% of top 1,000 websites block GPTBot (Cloudflare, Aug 2024). One robots.txt update unlocks all of ChatGPT's crawl access. Sites that fix this alone jump from 0 to 60+ on Crawler Access.

**2. Adding an llms.txt file (+10 pts)**
Fewer than 5% of sites have one. It takes 30 minutes to create. It tells AI systems exactly what your site is for and which pages matter — dramatically improving citation accuracy.

**3. Adding Organization JSON-LD schema (+14 pts)**
This tells AI systems who you are, not just what a page contains. Without it, AI models have to guess your brand's identity — and they often guess wrong.

These three fixes alone can move a site from a score of 29 to 55+.

For an agency, this is a one-afternoon client deliverable. Run the scan, implement the fixes, show the before/after score. That's a retainer renewal conversation.
