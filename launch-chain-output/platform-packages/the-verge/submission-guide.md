# The Verge — Submission Guide

IMPORTANT: The Verge does NOT accept guest posts. Only story pitches for their reporters.

1. Find the right reporter: look for recent Verge articles on AI search, SEO, or developer tools
2. Find their contact via Twitter/X or The Verge's staff page
3. Send story-pitch.md as a brief email or Twitter DM (DMs often work better)
4. Keep it to 150 words — reporters get many pitches; brevity is respect
5. Do NOT send attachments — paste everything inline
6. Follow up once after 1 week

NOTES:
- The Verge targets general tech-interested readers, not developers — frame the impact broadly
- The "invisible websites" angle is more compelling for their audience than the technical implementation
- If they write the story, it will be their work — you are a source, not the author
