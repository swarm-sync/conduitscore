# Nurture Email 1 — Checklist Delivery
**Sequence:** Post-Checklist Download Nurture
**Send Day:** Day 0 — Immediate send on checklist download (automated trigger)
**Audience:** Checklist download leads (website owners, SEO managers, indie consultants)
**Framework:** Hook-Story-Offer (HSO) — deliver value immediately, then bridge to the scan

---

## Subject Lines

**Primary (Subject A):** Your AI Visibility Checklist is here
**Variant (Subject B):** [FIRST_NAME], your checklist + one shortcut that saves 2 hours

*Subject A rationale: Direct delivery subject — recipient just downloaded something and expects this. Clean, functional, high open rate because it delivers on the implicit promise. Predicted open rate: 55–70% (post-download delivery emails have highest open rates in any sequence).*

*Subject B rationale: Personalization + benefit — adds intrigue to a pure delivery email by hinting at a shortcut. Use if testing shows Subject A has lower engagement after open.*
