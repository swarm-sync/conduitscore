85% of sites have no llms.txt file.

Without it, AI crawlers are navigating your site blind.

**Tweet 3:**
40% of sites have robots.txt configurations blocking AI crawlers.

Check yours right now: yourdomain.com/robots.txt

If you don't see explicit Allow rules for GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot — you're blocking them.

The fix is 4 lines:

User-agent: GPTBot
Allow: /

User-agent: ClaudeBot
Allow: /

**Tweet 4:**
70% of sites have no JSON-LD structured data beyond basic OG tags.

AI models use schema.org markup to build entity graphs. Without it, your content is unstructured noise.

At minimum: Organization + Article schemas on every content page.

**Tweet 5:**
60% of sites have no named author with a linked profile.

AI models weight sources by citation authority. A byline reading "Posted by Admin" scores worse than "By [Name], [Title] at [Company] | March 2024."

Your author page is an SEO asset you're probably ignoring.
