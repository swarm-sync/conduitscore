# LinkedIn — Submission Guide

## Option 1: Status Post (fastest, highest engagement)
1. Log in to LinkedIn
2. Click "Start a post"
3. Paste Version A or Version B from post.md (choose based on your audience mix)
4. Add a screenshot of a real scan result as the image
5. Post — no approval needed
6. Engage with every comment for the first 2 hours

## Option 2: LinkedIn Article (more durable, SEO value)
1. Click "Write article" on the LinkedIn home feed
2. Adapt the Hashnode article content for LinkedIn format
3. Add relevant hashtags: #SEO #AI #DigitalMarketing #ContentMarketing
4. Publish

## Hashtags for Status Post
#SEO #AI #ArtificialIntelligence #ContentMarketing #DigitalMarketing #AISearch #LLMs

## Best Posting Times
LinkedIn: Tuesday–Thursday, 8-10 AM or 5-6 PM (your timezone)
