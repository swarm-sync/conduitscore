### Full Description (800 words)
```
## The Problem
Your website is invisible to AI agents.

You don't get traffic from ChatGPT when people ask questions your
site could answer. Claude doesn't recommend you. Perplexity returns
your competitor's page instead.

You don't have a score for it. You don't know why. You need a
scorecard.

## The Solution: ConduitScore

ConduitScore is the first SaaS that measures how visible your
website is to AI agents. Think of it as "SEO for AI."

**Enter any URL → Get a 0-100 score + actionable fixes.**

### What You Get

1. **AI Visibility Score (0-100)**
   A single number measuring your site's discoverability across
   ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and other AI agents.

2. **7 Category Breakdowns**
   - Content Quality: Is your content novel, valuable, cited?
   - Structure: Is your site well-organized for AI parsing?
   - Metadata: Do you have proper schema, descriptions, tags?
   - Accessibility: Can AI agents crawl and understand your pages?
   - Freshness: When was your content last updated?
   - Links: Do other sites link to you (authority signal)?
   - Technical Setup: robots.txt, sitemap.xml, llms.txt, security.

3. **Specific Issues Identified**
   Not generic advice. Real findings:
   - "Missing structured data for your product schema"
   - "5 pages blocked in robots.txt that should be indexable"
   - "Thin content on /pricing (94 words, AI prefers 500+)"
   - "No blog refresh in 8 months — signals stale content"

4. **Copy-Paste Code Fixes**
   Each issue includes actual code to fix it:
   - JSON-LD schema snippet
   - robots.txt rule
   - HTML meta tag
   - Next.js/React component

   Plus estimated impact: "Fixing this issue will improve your
   score by 3-5 points."

5. **API Access** (Starter tier+)
   - Bulk scan multiple domains
   - Integrate scanning into your CI/CD pipeline
   - Scheduled scanning (weekly, monthly)
   - Webhooks for score change alerts

6. **Monitoring Dashboard**
   - Track your score over time
   - See which categories improved/declined
   - Monitor when new issues appear
   - Export reports for clients/stakeholders

## Why Now?

AI agents answer 1B+ queries daily. People are asking questions on
ChatGPT instead of Google. Your website needs visibility here.

Traditional SEO focuses on Google's single algorithm. AI agents have
different ranking factors:
- **Content freshness** matters more (AI prefers recent data)
- **Primary sources** valued higher (original research beats summaries)
- **Structured data** critical (AI needs to understand page meaning)
- **E-E-A-T** signals key (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness)

ConduitScore measures all of this so you can compete.
```
