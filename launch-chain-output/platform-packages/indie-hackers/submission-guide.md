# Indie Hackers — Submission Guide
1. Create/log in at https://www.indiehackers.com
2. Go to https://www.indiehackers.com/post/new
3. Choose post type: "Product launch" or general post
4. Paste post.md content
5. Add product: link to conduitscore.com and add it to your IH profile first
6. Choose relevant groups: #launched, #seo, #saas-products
7. Post — no approval queue
8. Engage with every comment for the first 24 hours
9. Consider doing a separate "Milestone" post when you hit $100 MRR and $1K MRR
