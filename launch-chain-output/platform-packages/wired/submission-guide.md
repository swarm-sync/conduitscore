# Wired — Submission Guide
1. Find relevant reporter via wired.com staff page or Twitter/X
2. Beat focus: AI, web, technology trends, developer ecosystem
3. Send pitch-email.md — keep to 200 words max
4. WIRED occasionally accepts contributor articles at: contribute@wired.com
   - Subject: "Contributor pitch: [title]"
   - Include writing samples and bio
5. Response time: 2-4 weeks
6. For reporter pitches: Twitter/X DM often faster than email
