# Product Hunt — Submission Guide

## Prerequisites
- [ ] Product Hunt account created and profile complete
- [ ] Identify a hunter (500+ followers) or post yourself
- [ ] Gallery images and thumbnail ready (see assets-brief.md)
- [ ] First comment drafted and ready to paste immediately at launch

## Submission Steps

1. Go to https://www.producthunt.com/posts/new
2. Enter URL: `https://conduitscore.com`
3. **Tagline** (paste from tagline.txt — 60 chars exactly):
   `AI Visibility Score — see if ChatGPT can find your website`
4. **Description** (paste from description.txt — 187 chars)
5. Upload thumbnail (240×240 PNG)
6. Upload gallery images: hero → fixes → dashboard → mobile
7. Set **Topics**: SEO, Artificial Intelligence, SaaS, Developer Tools, Content Marketing
8. Set launch date: **Tuesday or Wednesday**
9. Set launch time: **12:01 AM PST**

## Launch Day Checklist
- [ ] Post first-comment.md within first 5 minutes of going live
- [ ] Share PH link on Twitter/LinkedIn personally
- [ ] Post in relevant Slack communities (Indie Hackers, Online Geniuses)
- [ ] Ask genuine users (who have tried the product) to upvote
- [ ] Respond to every comment within 2 hours on launch day

## Timing Notes
- Best window: Tuesday–Thursday, 12:01 AM PST
- Avoid Mondays, Fridays, weekends
- Do NOT launch same day as major AI announcements (OpenAI, Google, Anthropic events)
- Launch Product Hunt AFTER assembling initial social proof (50+ beta scans, testimonials)

## Known Caveats
- Requires publicly accessible live product at conduitscore.com
- Only ask genuine users to upvote — PH penalizes vote rings
- The first comment is your most important asset — post it before any other comments appear
