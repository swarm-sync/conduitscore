# TechCrunch — Submission Guide
1. Find the relevant beat reporter (AI, SaaS, or developer tools) at TechCrunch
   Search: techcrunch.com — look for recent articles on AI search, SEO tools, or LLM visibility
2. Find editor email via LinkedIn or reporter bio page
3. Send pitch-email.md — do not attach files; embed everything in the body
4. Follow up once after 5 business days if no response
5. Do NOT pitch the same story to multiple TechCrunch reporters simultaneously

NOTES:
- TechCrunch prefers exclusives — offer a 48-hour exclusive window
- Lead with the data (85% missing llms.txt, 40% blocking AI bots) — these are your hook
- Include your personal credibility briefly (why are you the right person to have noticed this)
