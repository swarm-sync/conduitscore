# Cold Email 5 — The Close
**Sequence:** Agency Cold Outreach
**Send Day:** Day 10 (Thursday or Friday, 9–11am recipient's local timezone)
**Audience:** SEO agency owners and directors (2–20 employees)
**Framework:** HSO — Hook → Story → Offer (breakup email variant)

---

## Subject Lines

**Primary (Subject A):** Last note — free scan offer
**Variant (Subject B):** One client site. 15 seconds. That's all I'm asking.

*Subject A rationale: "Last note" signals this is the final email — triggers reciprocity and urgency without false deadlines. "Free scan" grounds the ask in zero-risk. Predicted open rate: 32–42% (breakup emails typically outperform earlier sequence emails).*

*Subject B rationale: Micro-commitment close — smallest possible ask framed as the only thing being requested. Removes all friction from the decision. Strong alternative for non-openers.*

---
